# Creative Director Review — Example Output

> This is what the creative director agent produces when reviewing slides
> against a brand system. Note the specificity — hex values, pixel measurements,
> and actionable direction.

---

## Slide 1 — Title

**What's working:**
- Strong editorial serif headline — bold, confident, magazine-like
- Near-black background delivers intended drama and contrast
- Amber underline gives personality, ties to brand accent
- Corner marks create consistent signature treatment

**What needs attention:**
- Background has slight texture — should be pure matte #0A0A0A
- Subtitle too large relative to headline — hierarchy flattened
- Amber underline weight feels too smooth/vector (needs hand-drawn quality)

**Direction for next iteration:**
- Reduce subtitle size by 20-30%, increase letter-spacing slightly
- Lock to 6-column Swiss grid with 80-100px outer margins
- Replace underline with scanned hand-drawn stroke, amber ~#F2A046, 4-6px weight
- Align attribution to headline's left grid column

---

## Slide 4 — Comparison Bars

**What's working:**
- Clear two-row comparison communicates the insight quickly
- Headline consistent with the system
- Amber circle draws attention to key data point

**What needs attention:**
- Teal elements break the amber-only accent rule — introduces competing color
- Bar label contrast too low for readability
- Hatching pattern reduces editorial clarity

**Direction for next iteration:**
- Remove teal — replace with neutral grey for functional graphics, amber for accents only
- Flat fills only (no patterns) — light grey bars, white endpoint blocks
- Align all labels to 12px baseline grid, vertically center in bars
- Use amber hand-drawn arrow consistent with other accent strokes

---

## Cross-Slide Consistency Notes

- **Color:** Restrict decorative accent to single warm amber (#F2A046). Eliminate secondary accent colors.
- **Typography:** Serif for all headlines. One sans family for all body/subhead/labels. Standardize sizes across slides.
- **Grid:** Lock every slide to consistent 6-column Swiss grid + 8-12px baseline.
- **Style:** Remove all textures, glows, shadows, gradients. Flat fills and consistent strokes only.
- **Accessibility:** Check all copy for 4.5:1 contrast ratio. Body line lengths 45-60 characters.
